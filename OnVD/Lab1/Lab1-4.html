Computer science is a field that deals with the study of computation, programming languages, algorithms, data structures, and computer systems. It involves the theory, design, development, and application of computer software and hardware. Computer science is a rapidly evolving field with a wide range of applications in various industries such as healthcare, finance, transportation, and entertainment. Professionals in this field work to create innovative technologies that improve the way we live, work, and communicate.

FINKI, which stands for Faculty of Computer Science and Engineering, is a renowned educational institution located in Skopje, North Macedonia. Established in 2001, FINKI offers undergraduate and graduate programs in computer science, software engineering, and information systems. The faculty is dedicated to providing students with high-quality education and practical training, using state-of-the-art technologies and modern teaching methods. FINKI also conducts research in various areas of computer science, collaborating with academic and industry partners both nationally and internationally. With its strong academic reputation and commitment to innovation, FINKI is a leading institution in the region for computer science and engineering education.
